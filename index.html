Since DGEMS'23, many of the Select 200 companies went on to successfully raise funds & expanded rapidly to international markets.

The Forbes India ID Globalist Entrepreneur Mobility Summit (DGEMS) has become the world's first cohort dedicated for spotting 200 future global brands, and helping them with an access to global markets and investors.

Bringing together some of the most innovative and scalable companies, sharing a global vision together, the DGEMS cohort focuses on the unique concept of "eXtrepreneurs" i.e. A limitless entrepreneur, with a global mindset, doing business beyond the native boundaries". Here, we delve into the inspiring journeys of a few select eXtrepreneurs and their companies that have turned their DGEMS recognition into global triumphs.

Engagement with the DGEMS community provided us access to a diverse group of industry peers. Participation in the DGEMS forum has also elevated our brand visibility within the tech and retail sectors. Sharing our success stories and insights gained through the development. It has positioned Innoviti as a thought leader, attracting interest from potential clients and collaborators. In essence, the DGEMS forum has not only been a support system but also a vital resource in Innoviti's quest to redefine the retail payment landscape. It provided a fertile ground for innovation, col- laboration, and growth, aligning perfectly with our mission to deliver seamless and secure payment solutions that cater to the evolving needs of businesses and consumers alike. "DGEMS cohort serves as a unique forum for global collaborations amongst the fast-growing 200 companies sharing the common goal of creating global brands. As we celebrate the achievement of many of these companies, we look forward to witnessing their continued growth and adding more stories to the Select 200 Story board." Divesh Sharma, Founder & CEO, D Globalist

For more detailed insights and to explore collaboration opportunities with these trailblazing companies, visit our DGEMS Success Stories Pace.
